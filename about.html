About CueBallPath

CueBallPath is a free interactive billiards training platform designed to help pool players of all skill levels improve their game through visualization and calculation tools.

Our Tools

  • Aiming Calculator — Visualize ghost ball position and cut angles for any shot
  • Diamond System Solver — Calculate 1-6 rail kick shots using the mirror image method
  • Position Planner — Plan cue ball movement with follow, stun, and draw stroke predictions
  • English (Sidespin) Visualizer — See how left/right spin affects cue ball path off rails

How It Works

Our tools use real billiards physics including the Resting Energy Requirement formulas for ball deflection, rail reflection angles, and spin transfer mechanics. All calculations happen instantly in your browser — no downloads or accounts needed.

Who It's For

  • Beginners learning how to aim and control the cue ball
  • Intermediate players working on kick shots and position play
  • Coaches demonstrating shot mechanics to students
  • Advanced players studying multi-rail solutions
